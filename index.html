Our Story

A home-cooked corner of Big Grove Township.

Newark's Country Kitchen has been family-owned and family-run since 1990. We're the diner you stop at on the way back from the farm. The one where the coffee's hot before you sit down and the eggs come out the way you ordered them.

Nothing fancy. Nothing fussy. Just the kind of plate you wish your grandma still made for you on a Saturday morning — biscuits and sausage gravy, pancakes the size of the plate, ham steak with two over easy.

“Pull up a chair. Coffee's on us while you decide.”
